Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Apex ("we", "our", "the app") is an open-source job search tool built by IDYeah Studio LLC. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Email address — used for authentication only. We do not send marketing emails.
  • Profile data you provide — resume, target roles, locations, writing voice, and preferences. This is stored so the agent can search and draft on your behalf.
  • Job search results — titles, companies, locations, and URLs found during LinkedIn searches.
  • AI-generated content — scores, cover letters, emails, and LinkedIn notes generated by Claude for your review.
  • LinkedIn cookie (optional) — if you provide your li_at cookie, it is stored encrypted in the database and used only to authenticate LinkedIn searches on your behalf.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not track your browsing activity.
  • We do not use analytics, cookies (beyond authentication), or third-party trackers.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

How your data is stored

All data is stored in a Supabase database. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth. Data is isolated per user — you can only access your own profile and jobs.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI scoring and drafting. Your resume and job descriptions are sent to Claude for processing. (privacy policy)
  • LinkedIn — public job listings are scraped. No data is sent to LinkedIn beyond search queries.
  • Render — hosting (privacy policy)

Data retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your profile and all associated data at any time from the Settings page, or by contacting us.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing us at the address below.

Open source

Apex is open source under the MIT License. You can self-host your own instance, in which case your data never touches our servers. See GitHub.
